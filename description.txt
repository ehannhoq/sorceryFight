Sorcery Fight is heavily inspired by anime/manga Jujutsu Kaisen.

The mod adds:
Two innate techniques, Limitless and Shrine
each with a total of 10 cursed techniques, 5 passive techniques, and 2 domain expansions.

NOTE: This mod requires Calamity Mod and its dependencies in order to install.
