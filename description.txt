Sorcery Fight is heavily inspired by anime/manga Jujutsu Kaisen. It adds 10 Cursed Technqiues, 5 Passive Techniques, and 2 Domain Expansions.

NOTE: This mod requires Calamity Mod and its dependencies in order to install.

Due to popular demand, a wiki for the mod is in the works! Thank you for downloading and playing Sorcery Fight!
