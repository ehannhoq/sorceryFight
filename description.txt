Sorcery Fight is heavily inspired by anime/manga Jujutsu Kaisen.

The mod adds:
Four innate techniques, Limitless, Shrine, Private Pure Love Train, Vessel, and the 'Physically Gifted' Heavenly Restriction,
for a total of 22 cursed techniques, 7 passive techniques, and 4 domain expansions, 
as well as 2 custom boss domains!

NOTE: This mod requires Calamity Mod and its dependencies in order to install.
