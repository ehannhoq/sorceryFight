Sorcery Fight is heavily inspired by anime/manga Jujutsu Kaisen. It adds one Innate Technique, five Cursed Technqiues, three Passive Techniques, and one Domain Expansion.

NOTE: This mod requires Calamity Mod and its dependencies in order to install.
